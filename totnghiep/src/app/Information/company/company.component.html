About Us

Tan Hoa 1 Co., Ltd. was established in 2001 and the factory is located in Dong Nai with area of 11,000 m2. Inside company, We have an own canteen and houses for workers, so our human resources are very stable. There are many workers and staffs who have worked for more than 10 years. Also, we always care about social responsibility and in order to maintain this, we have participated BSCI from November 2017. We care not only about society but also about the environment . Therefore, we has trained about using legal woods and finally we got FSC COC in 2016. Our major business is producing outdoor furniture and main materials are wood, metal, stone table top, rope and wicker. All items will have internal test and pass ( base on EN581 standard). And all items can pass REACH ( chemical test). We also have experience about making products with mail order packing, ISTA/3A and ISTA/6A. Our products have exported to USA, EU, UK, Korea and Japan. We always welcome all customers from around the world to my factory showroom to experience our product designs.
